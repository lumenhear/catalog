Speech & Language Disorders

Articulation Disorder

Difficulty producing specific speech sounds correctly, often substituting or omitting sounds.

Phonological Disorder

Patterns of sound errors that affect entire classes of sounds, making speech difficult to understand.

Childhood Apraxia of Speech

Motor speech disorder where the brain has difficulty coordinating the movements needed for speech.
